## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I don't have experience in education?
A: No problem! We provide comprehensive training for you and your facilitators. Many successful franchisees came from different backgrounds.

### Q: Can I modify the curriculum?
A: The core TechDojo curriculum is standardized to maintain quality across all branches. However, you can add supplementary programs tailored to your local market.

### Q: How long does it take to break even?
A: This depends on your location, marketing efforts, and student enrollment. We provide financial projections during the franchising process.

### Q: What support do you provide after launch?
A: Ongoing support includes weekly coaching calls, access to our AI platform, curriculum updates, marketing materials, and a community of franchisees for peer learning.

### Q: What if I need to contact someone urgently?
A: For urgent matters, reach out to 0995-836-2249 for an orientation call. For general inquiries, email 3DBotics.LC@gmail.com.
