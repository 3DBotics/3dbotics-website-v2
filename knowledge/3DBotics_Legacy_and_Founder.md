## 🚀 The Birth of 3DBotics®
3DBotics® was born out of a "Devastating to Divine" turnaround. 

1. **The Attic Farm:** During the 2020 pandemic lockdown, Veni started the biggest 3D Printing farm in the Philippines in the attic of his own home. 
2. **The Evolution:** What started as a farm evolved into **3DBotics®**—the Philippines' only IPOPHL-certified and trademarked integration of 3D Printing, AI, and Modern Robotics.
3. **Rapid Growth:** In just one year, the vision expanded from a single attic to **over 50 branches** nationwide.
